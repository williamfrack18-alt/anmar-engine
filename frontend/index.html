Frequently Asked Questions.

Do I keep full ownership of what we build?

100%. Everything built on Anmar is yours from day one — the code, the brand, the product. We operate as a strategic partner, not a co-owner.

How fast can I go from idea to launch?

Most projects go live in 2–4 weeks. Our Supra AI engine cuts development time by up to 90% — what used to take a 10-person team six months, we deliver in weeks.

How does the funding work?

Once your product is live and showing traction, our capital committee reviews the numbers. If it checks out, we move fast — direct investment, third-party capital, or credit lines. No pitch required.

What exactly does my subscription include?

Marketing ($297/mo) covers paid campaigns, creatives, and a community manager. Marketing + Build ($397/mo) adds the full engineering team. Your team is active from day one.

What does the Build phase actually deliver?

A real, functional product — not a mockup. Live previews, chat-based iterations with the engineering team, and a production-ready codebase you own completely.

What if my idea isn't fully defined yet?

That's what the first chat is for. We guide you through defining the problem, the audience, and the solution before a single line of code is written.

When does the capital track open?

When your project has real, demonstrable traction. At that point, you unlock access to Anmar's investment committee and third-party funding options — all inside the platform.
